Problems

Results

Here is our pseudocode as a list and as JS comments:

How to plant an oak tree:

  1. Collect acorns.
  2. Determine viable acorns:
    1. Place acorns in cold water.
    2. Discard acorns that float to the top.
  3. Fill small pots with potting mix.
  4. Plant two acorns sideways in each pot, one inch deep.
  5. Cover pots with screen.
  6. Keep the soil moist (leave them be in winter)
  7. If both acorns germinate, cut off the weaker one.
  8. When seedlings outgrow their pots, transfer them to 2 qt. nursery pots.
  9. Once saplings outgrow their pots, plant the tree in the ground:
    1. Loosen root ball and trim any kinked roots.
    2. Dig a 4 ft. wide hole as deep as the root ball.
    3. Replace soil on top of the root ball
  10. Water every week until tree matures
